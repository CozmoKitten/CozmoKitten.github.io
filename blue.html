Figure Skating

Figure Skating is an hobby of mine. While some people see figure skating as a boring thing- most not conserdering it not a sport- but to me, its more then that. I've been skating since I was 4, progressing from 2-bladed skates, up to figure skates. Personally, I find figure skating freeing- allowing you to let go of the outside world, and flow across the ice, just-- enjoying yourself. While figure skating might sometimes be boring to watch, a great preformance is interesting. With a flow of huge jumps, flying 10ft across the ice- spins, with the possibility of 350RPM, on ice! Overall, to me, figure skating is more then a sport, its an escape to a fun, relaxing world.
